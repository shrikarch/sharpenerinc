VOXPOP

Launched a year back, Vox Pop Clothing is an ecommerce portal that offers authentic apparel of pop culture icons. True to its name, it is a collage of unique expressions, designed for people who can speak their mind and wear their attitude. A sub section of www.voxpopclothing.com, 'Voxpress' is a platform where brands and designers can earn profit by simply uploading their t-shirt design on their website.
To communicate the same, we used stop motion, an animation style that brought to life everyday design ideas that transform into different interesting objects and finally get converted to a cool Tshirt design.
