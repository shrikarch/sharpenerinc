KAILASH PARBAT

It doesn’t matter if you’re a pukka Mumbaikar or a true-blue South Bombay denizen. If you are familiar with the flavours of Mumbai, you would surely have savoured the fare at Kailash Parbat at some point in your life. The management was seeking a fresh look that would infuse a sense of the contemporary into their brand. One that would be in sync with the feel and ambience of their new restaurant.
We created a website that would reflect Kailash Parbat's modern, new avatar. The site aims to engage the consumer with every click, through an interactive experience backed by a simple, yet attractive look. The home page is our rendition of the new restaurant's interiors, using a classy, contemporary style of illustration.
