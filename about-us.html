Hetal Ajmera has nearly 12 years of advertising experience, having worked at Leo Burnett, Ogilvy, JWT and most recently Contract Advertising as Senior Creative Director. Over these years, she has brought her unique brand of art direction and design to many a prestigious client and project – Fevicol, Orange (Vodafone), Lux, Sunsilk, Cadbury’s, Asian Paints, Dove, DeBeers, Shoppers Stop and Times of India, to name but a few.
A gold medalist in Commercial Art from Sophia Polytechnic, Hetal has always had a penchant for creativity. Her mantra is simple: “Design is not about fitting in. It’s about standing out.” This philosophy has resulted in her being declared ‘Art Director of the Year’ in 2008. Her work has been recognized and awarded at shows both national and international, including Young Guns, Adfest Asia Pacific, Clio and Goafest. Recently, India’s first design magazine Creative Gaga profiled Hetal, showcasing her contribution to the world of design.

Bianca D’sa has 6 years of experience in the field of art and design. She started out at McCann Erickson and went on to Contract Advertising, and has worked on several reputed brands as diverse as Saffola, Gems and Jewellery Council of India, Nerolac, Asian Paints, HSBC, American Tourister, Samsonite, Shoppers Stop and Cadbury Celebrations.
In a short span, Bianca has evolved and honed her craft tremendously, and the numerous awards she has won bear testimony to this. Her work has been acclaimed both nationally and internationally, at shows like Spikes Asia, Goafest and Effies. She was also the recipient of the Yahoo Big Idea award in 2013.
